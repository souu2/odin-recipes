Lasagna Recipe

Description of Lasagna

This is a recipe on how to make a delicious lasagne

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pastry
  • Mince Beef
  • Tomata Paste

Steps

  1. Cook beef mince first
  2. Put the beef between the Lasagna Pastry
  3. Put tomato paste on top of beef mince
  4. Repeat how many layers you want
  5. Put in oven to cook
