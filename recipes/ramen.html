Ramen Recipe

Description of Ramen

This is how to make Ramen

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Beef Broth
  • Meat of your choice

Steps

  1. Cook Ramen noodles to your liking
  2. Then cook the meat of your choice
  3. Put the beef broth into noodles
  4. Put cooked meat on top
